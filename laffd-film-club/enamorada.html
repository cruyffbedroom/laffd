Enamorada

In Mexican Revolution times, a guerrilla general (Armendáriz) and his troops take the conservative town of Cholula, near by Mexico City. As the revolutionaries mistreat the town’s riches, Armendáriz falls for beautiful and wild Beatriz Peñafiel.

Enamorada

Mexico / 99 min / 1946 / fiction
in Spanish with English subtitles
Direction: Emilio Fernández
Screenplay: Emilio Fernández, Íñigo de Martino
Cinematography: Gabriel Figueroa
Editing: Gloria Schoemann
Producers: Benito Alazraki
Cast: María Félix, Pedro Armendáriz, Fernando Fernández, Miguel Inclán
