The Major Tones

In Ingrid Pokropek’s magical realist feature debut, lonely 14-year-old Ana has had a metal plate in her arm since a childhood accident. But when that plate starts picking up messages, vibrating in Morse code, Ana and a young soldier must decipher their meaning.

The Major Tones

Los tonos mayores
Argentina / 101 min / 2023 / fiction
Texas Premiere
in Spanish with English subtitles
Direction: Ingrid Pokropek
Screenplay: Ingrid Pokropek
Cinematography: Ana Roy
Editing: Miguel de Zuviría
Producers: Iván Moscovich, Juan Segundo Álamos, Ingrid Pokropek, Magdalena Schavelzon, Pablo Piedras, Miguel Molina, Adán Aliaga
Cast: Sofía Clausen, Pablo Seijo, Lina Ziccarello, Santiago Ferreira, Mercedes Halfon, Walter Jakob

Trailer

The experience of a young girl maneuvering her way around Buenos Aires is made fantastic under the gaze of the director, Ingrid Pokropek. There is a tenderness in the film that makes the small blows that the protagonist suffers resonate even harder. The viewer wants to protect this young girl, perhaps, but we also want to see her spin further and further into the mystery that drives her. What is her body telling her? And how will this transform her? Perhaps adolescence is a question of listening the revolution happening inside of your body, giving into that mystery... - Jhon Hernandez
