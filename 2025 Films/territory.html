Territory

Roman, who is dedicated to protecting political leaders and trying to develop as a boxing trainer and manager, is harassed by a duo of abusive representatives who have in turn been hired as shock troops by an opposing political party.

Territory

Territorio
Argentina / 89 min / 2024 / fiction
US Premiere
in Spanish with English subtitles
Direction: José Celestino Campusano
Screenplay: José Celestino Campusano
Cinematography: Federico Jacobi
Editing: Horacio Florentin
Producers: José Celestino Campusano
Cast: Gustavo Vieyra, Farid Herrera, Christian Alejandro Gauto, Juan Marcos Fernandez

Trailer

Conflicts arise everywhere because there is not a single area in this character's life that does not contain the possibility of an explosion. Everyone at some point wants to deceive him, they attack him, they have him in their sights. The main reason for his problems is that he is a man of codes in a world that no longer has them. You may not even be able to make your child understand that small but vital teaching. Affiliation is a clear theme of the film, and this impossibility of transmitting wisdom from generation to generation reveals the degradation of relationships in a community in which respect no longer exists. Politics, sports, friendship, love: everything has become an excuse for selfishness and a battlefield in which only the worst survive. - Lautaro Garcia Candela and Lucas Granero
