LAFFD 2024

LAFFD is currently putting together its inaugural program. We aim to scour for the best of current Latin American cinema that has not come to DFW. It's our hope that with this initial offering we can give a taste of what's going on in this truly vibrant film scene.

Retrospective Screenings

An essential part of any festival is how it deals with film history. It's our hope to bring some retrospective screenings in our lineup that shows the incredible history and achievement of Latin American cinema.
