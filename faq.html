LAFFD 2024

What is LAFFD?
The Latin American Film Festival of Dallas is dedicated to bringing the best of Latin American filmmaking to DFW.

When is LAFFD?
The festival will take place from February 29th to March 4th.

Where is the festival taking place?
All screenings are being hosted at the microcinema, Spacy, which is inside Tyler Station in Oak Cliff.
This is the address: 1300 S Polk St #160A, Dallas, TX 75224

It seems kind of hard to find?
Please follow the signs to Spacy at the entrance, next to Oak Cliff Bike Synergy. If you’re attending a screening after 8pm, please enter through the Oak Cliff Brewing Co.

How is the parking situation?
It is recommended to use the parking lot through the “South Entry,” but there is additional parking at the “North Entry.”

How much are tickets?
All tickets are $10 and are final sale

Can I buy tickets at the door?
First, check online to see if tickets are still available. Box office opens 30 minutes prior to the first screening of the day.

Are there festival passes?
No, each ticket must be individually purchased.

What countries are represented?
We have 11 films from 7 countries. The countries are Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Mexico and the United States.

Do the films have English subtitles?
Yes, all films are screening with English subtitles.

For any additional questions and concerns, please reach out to us at laffdprogram@gmail.com
